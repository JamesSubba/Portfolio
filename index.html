Cube-Runner is a 3D game developed using the Unity Game engine and CSharp. In this game, there is a red cube and the entire goal of this game is eat as many other red cube as possible and avoid the blue and green cube. If the red cube hits green or blue cube the game will be over and the score will be displayed on a card along with quit and reset
